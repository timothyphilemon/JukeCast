TV Installation Guide

Get JukeCast running on your Fire TV or Android TV in a few minutes.

What you'll need
Fire TV or Android TV
Any Amazon Fire TV Stick, Fire TV Cube, or Android TV device with developer options enabled.
SmartTube (free)
An ad-free YouTube player for TV. JukeCast uses this to play videos. Install it before JukeCast.
Your session ID
Create a TV in your dashboard first to get your 6-digit session ID — you'll need it during setup.
Step-by-step setup
1
Enable Developer Options on your Fire TV
Go to Settings → My Fire TV → About and click the Fire TV name 7 times rapidly. You'll see "You are now a developer" appear. Then go back to My Fire TV → Developer Options and turn on ADB Debugging and Apps from Unknown Sources.
2
Install SmartTube
On your Fire TV, open the Downloader app (install it free from the Amazon store if you don't have it). Enter this URL and install:
https://smarttubeapp.github.io
Follow the on-screen prompts to complete the SmartTube installation.
3
Install JukeCast
Still in the Downloader app, enter the JukeCast APK URL and install it:
https://jukecast.tv/jukecast.apk
When prompted, tap Install then Done (not Open).
4
Open JukeCast and enter your session ID
Find JukeCast in your apps and open it. On first launch you'll be asked for your 6-digit session ID — enter the one from your venue dashboard. This connects the TV to your account permanently. You won't be asked again.
5
You're live
Your dashboard will show a ● Connected status for that TV. Customers can now scan a QR code or go to jukecast.tv and enter your session ID to start casting.
✓
JukeCast runs quietly in the background. When a customer queues a video it wakes up, plays it, then returns to whatever app was running — Pluto TV, Tubi, Atmosphere TV, or whatever you set in your dashboard.
Troubleshooting
TV shows "Not connected" in dashboard
Make sure the JukeCast app is open and running on the TV, and that the TV has an active internet connection.
Videos won't play
Make sure SmartTube is installed. Some YouTube videos block third-party playback — try a different video or a music video from an official channel.
Wrong session ID entered on TV
Uninstall and reinstall the JukeCast app — the setup screen will appear again on first launch so you can enter the correct ID.
Can't install from Downloader
Double-check that Apps from Unknown Sources is enabled in Developer Options. You may also need to enable it specifically for the Downloader app.
