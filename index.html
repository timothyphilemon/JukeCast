FAQ

What types of videos can be played?

Customers can play music videos, sports clips, live performances, and more using YouTube content. You remain in control with moderation and content settings.

How long does setup take?

Setup typically takes just a few minutes per TV. Once installed, it runs in the background with no ongoing effort required.

Do I need new equipment to use JukeCast?

No. JukeCast works with your existing smart TVs, Fire TV, or Android TV setup. We handle a simple one-time installation so you're ready to go.

Do customers need to download an app?

No. Customers simply scan a QR code and use their phone browser. There's nothing to download.

Will this interrupt my normal TV content?

Only temporarily. When a customer plays a video, it briefly takes over the screen, then your normal content automatically resumes afterward.

Can I control what gets played?

Yes. You can filter the type of content that plays, with built-in moderation tools to keep everything appropriate.

How do I make money from this?

Customers pay to play videos or playlists on your screens. You earn a portion of every transaction automatically.

Do my staff need to manage anything?

No. The entire system runs automatically. Customers control everything from their phone, and your staff doesn't need to be involved.

What if I want to play my own content?

You can display your own content at any time. JukeCast does not interfere with your normal setup and resumes your content automatically after each play.
